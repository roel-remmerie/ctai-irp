## [Energy-efficient altitude optimization in multi-UAV search and rescue: A hybrid swarm approach](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2542660525002264?via%3Dihub)

- What is the problem and how is the research approached?
    - Minimizing the number of UAV's necessary in a SAR operation
    - A hybrid optimization approach tuning many hyperparameters
- Which key concepts are central and how are they defined?
    - system model
    - uav operations
- What are the results and conclusions?
    - the higher the drones search, the larger the area they can see becomes and thus fewer dornes are needed, however when flying too high the drones need more power for their ascent.
    - Genetic algorithms and particle swarm optimization is the best at optimizing the number of UAVs required for large area coverage, making it the most efficient solution for real-world USAR UAV operations in both regular and irregular terrains.
- What are the strengths and weaknesses?
    - positive, this study looks at a lot àof important prameters for search and rescue
    - negative, due to the large number of parameters the research is hard to follow
